Insurance Claim Automation

The ability to deal quickly with insurance claims is core for your business success in terms of high customer satisfaction and low claims cost. Our Insurance Claims Automation Solution supports efficient processing of electronic claim submissions.

Customers often submit claims by email. The initial actions of the insurance agent after receiving such a claim message are the following:

Extract the name and policy number from the message text

Match it with an existing entry in the customer database in a unique manner

Check if the claim is covered by the the insurance policy

One observers that the customers often forget to include the insurance policy number in the request or use name abbriviation. This often makes it hard to uniquely identify the customer and requires a reply with clarification request. Also every claim submission is also connected with a mundane process of checking of the claim validity, i.e if the claim is covered by an existing insurance agreement.

Meet RoboClaim

RoboClaim automates the way of validating the claim request, i.e if ensuring is complete, allows to match the the auto-match of extracted information with entries and allows to detect invalid claim.
